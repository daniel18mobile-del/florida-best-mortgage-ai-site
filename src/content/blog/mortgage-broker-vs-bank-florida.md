A bank lender generally offers that institution's mortgage products. A mortgage broker can help compare options from multiple lending sources, depending on licensing, relationships, borrower profile, and program availability.

The practical value is choice. More than one path may be available for the same buyer, property, and goal.

## Why Borrowers Compare Options

Mortgage choices can differ by rate, fees, guideline flexibility, documentation, property type, lock timing, closing speed, and communication style.

For Florida buyers, property-specific details such as condo review, insurance, flood zones, and appraisal can also affect which lender is the best fit.

## What to Ask Any Mortgage Professional

Ask which programs are being compared, what fees apply, what could change the payment, what documents are needed, and what property issues may affect approval.
