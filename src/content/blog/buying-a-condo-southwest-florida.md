Condo buying in Southwest Florida can be a great fit, but the mortgage review is different from a single-family home. The lender may need to review both the borrower and the condominium project.

That means association documents, insurance, reserves, budget, occupancy, litigation, and ownership details may matter.

## Why Early Condo Review Helps

Waiting too long to review the condo project can create stress close to the financing deadline. Ask for association documents early and tell your mortgage advisor the property is a condo as soon as possible.

Different loan programs may review condo projects differently, so the right path depends on the full file.

## Local Condo Questions

Naples, Bonita Springs, Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Estero, and Marco Island each have condos with different insurance, age, fee, and association profiles. Waterfront and older buildings may require extra attention.
