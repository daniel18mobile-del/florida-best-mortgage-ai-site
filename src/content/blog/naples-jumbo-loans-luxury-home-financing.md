Naples buyers often need jumbo financing for higher-value homes, waterfront property, luxury condos, or second homes. These loans may require a deeper review than a standard mortgage.

The right lender and program can depend on income type, assets, reserves, occupancy, property type, insurance, and appraisal details.

## Jumbo Review Items

Expect lenders to look closely at income, credit, down payment, reserves, assets, appraisal, title, insurance, and whether the property is a condo, second home, or investment property.

Self-employed buyers or buyers with complex assets may want to compare traditional jumbo with Non-QM options.

## Naples Property Questions

Luxury homes, waterfront properties, condos, and golf-community homes can involve unique insurance, HOA, condo, and appraisal questions. Early review helps protect the timeline.
