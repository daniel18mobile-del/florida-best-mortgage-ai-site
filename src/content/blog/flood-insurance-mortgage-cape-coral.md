Cape Coral buyers should talk about flood insurance early, especially when considering canal, waterfront, or low-lying properties. Insurance can affect the monthly payment and the lender's escrow review.

Flood zone details do not mean a home cannot be financed, but they can affect cost and documentation.

## What Lenders May Review

A lender may need proof of required homeowners and flood insurance, acceptable coverage, escrow details, and confirmation that the property meets program requirements.

If the insurance quote is much higher than expected, it can affect payment comfort and qualification.

## What Buyers Should Ask

Ask whether the property is in a flood zone, what insurance may be required, whether the seller has current policy information, and how the premium affects estimated monthly payment.
