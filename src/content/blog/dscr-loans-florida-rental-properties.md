DSCR loans are commonly used by real estate investors because the review focuses heavily on whether the rental property income can support the mortgage payment under lender rules.

They are not the right fit for every investor, but they can be useful when traditional income documentation does not match the investment strategy.

## What DSCR Lenders May Review

Common items include rent estimates, lease terms, appraisal rent schedules, credit, down payment, reserves, property type, title structure, and short-term rental restrictions.

Each lender may calculate DSCR differently, so comparing options matters.

## Florida Investor Questions

Investors in Naples, Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Bonita Springs, Estero, Marco Island, Ave Maria, Lehigh Acres, and Golden Gate should review HOA rules, insurance, taxes, rental demand, and cash-flow assumptions before choosing a loan.
