Jumbo financing is common in higher-value Florida markets, especially for waterfront homes, luxury condos, second homes, and buyers with more complex income or asset profiles.

Maria helps Florida buyers compare jumbo options and understand documentation, reserves, payment comfort, and property review before moving forward.

## Jumbo Loan Considerations

Jumbo lenders may have different requirements for credit, down payment, reserves, debt-to-income ratio, appraisal review, condo eligibility, and post-closing assets. The best loan path depends on the full file.

For self-employed buyers or buyers with substantial assets, it can be helpful to compare traditional jumbo options with Non-QM alternatives.

## Naples and Southwest Florida Jumbo Buyers

Naples, Marco Island, Bonita Springs, and waterfront areas of Cape Coral and Fort Myers often involve property-specific questions about insurance, flood zones, HOA dues, condo associations, and seasonal occupancy. Reviewing these details early can protect the timeline.
