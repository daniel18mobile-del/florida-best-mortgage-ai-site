Renovation financing may help a buyer or homeowner include eligible repairs or improvements in the overall mortgage plan. The right structure depends on the property, project, contractor, timeline, and loan program.

Maria can help you compare renovation financing with alternatives such as HELOC, home equity loan, cash-out refinance, or paying for improvements separately.

## Renovation Details Lenders May Review

Common review items include the scope of work, contractor credentials, bids, permits, appraisal details, after-improved value, draw process, inspections, and project completion timing.

Not every repair or improvement fits every program, so early review is important before assuming the numbers will work.

## Florida Renovation Planning

Insurance, flood zones, wind mitigation, roof age, permits, and condo or HOA rules can affect renovation plans in Southwest Florida. These items should be part of the financing conversation.
