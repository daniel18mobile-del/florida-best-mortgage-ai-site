## HELOC vs. Home Equity Loan

A HELOC may work well when you need flexible access over time. A home equity loan may fit better when you know the amount you need and prefer a predictable payment structure.

Both options depend on your available equity, credit, income, existing mortgage, property type, and lender guidelines. Borrowing against your home increases your debt and should be reviewed carefully.

## Questions to Review

Before choosing an equity product, consider how much you need, how quickly you will use the funds, whether the payment can change, and how the new debt fits your monthly budget.
