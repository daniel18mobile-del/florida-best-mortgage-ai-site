Non-QM loans can help some Florida borrowers who do not fit a standard conventional, FHA, or VA path. These programs may consider alternative documentation, investment-property income, assets, or other file details.

Florida Best Mortgage helps borrowers compare Non-QM with traditional programs so they understand cost, documentation, tradeoffs, and long-term plans.

## Common Non-QM Scenarios

Non-QM may be worth discussing for self-employed buyers, bank-statement income, 1099 income, asset-based qualification, recent credit events, investors, DSCR scenarios, and buyers with multiple properties.

Because pricing and guidelines vary, comparing more than one path matters. A Non-QM loan should be chosen because it fits the situation, not because it sounds easier.

## Florida Property Review Still Matters

Even with flexible documentation, the property must work for the lender. Condo eligibility, insurance, appraisal, occupancy, reserves, and title structure can all affect approval.
